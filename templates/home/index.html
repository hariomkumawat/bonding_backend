Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Love Bonding App

How does Love Bonding App work?

Love Bonding App provides daily activities and challenges designed for couples. You and your partner sign up, connect your profiles, and start completing activities together. Each activity helps you communicate better, have fun, and deepen your connection.

Can we use it if we're in a long-distance relationship?

Absolutely! Love Bonding App is perfect for long-distance couples. Many activities can be completed remotely, and you can share your responses and progress with each other even when apart.

What if my partner and I have different schedules?

No problem! Activities can be completed in Solo Mode where each partner completes them at their own pace, or Together Mode for real-time synchronization when you're both available.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes! Your data is encrypted and completely private. Only you and your partner can see your activity responses and relationship information. We never share your personal data with third parties.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your premium subscription at any time. You'll continue to have premium access until the end of your billing period, after which you'll revert to the free plan.
